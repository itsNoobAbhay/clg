About Institute

Shetkari Shikshan Sanstha’s College of Management Studies and Commerce is located in the heart of Navi Mumbai at Ghansoli. Shetkari Shikshan Sanstha’s College of Management Studies and Commerce was established in the year 2007 with two full-time undergraduate programs. The College offers 3-year integrated courses in Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) & Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS). The courses are affiliated with Mumbai University.

The Institute aims to provide all-round development to the students, where they not only achieve various skills but also learn to have the right attitude in all spheres of life. On joining the college, the students become a part of a strong and lively community, working within a framework that provides a stimulating and supportive environment for the exchange of ideas and intellectual development.

We focus not merely on academic excellence, but also on values such as courtesy, integrity, and cleanliness, thus ensuring that our students become good and ethical human beings.
