ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Information Technology at Ramniranjan Jhunjhunwala College, Ghatkopar, Mumbai was established in 2007. The Department offers both undergraduate (B.Sc. IT) and postgraduate (M.Sc. IT) programmes. The M.Sc IT programme was introduced in the year 2016.

B.Sc I.T

BSc. IT:

BSc. IT is a three years full time course which is designed around the core practices currently followed in IT industry.

Eligibility: A candidate for being eligible for admission to the degree course of Bachelor of Science – Information Technology , shall have passed XII Standard Examination of the Maharashtra Board of Higher Secondary Education or its equivalent with  Mathematics as one of the subjects.

OR

Candidates who have passed Diploma in Computer Engineering/Computer Science/ Computer Technology / Information Technology / Electrical, Electronic and Allied Branches, Mechanical and Allied Branches, Civil and Allied Branches of Engineering after S.S.C. (Xth) are Eligible for admission to direct admission to the Second Year of the B.Sc.(IT) degree course. However, the Diploma should be recognised by the Board of Technical Education or any other recognized Government body.

AND

Students with post H.S.C. – Diploma in Computer Engineering / Computer Science / Computer Technology / Information Technology and allied branches will be eligible for direct admission to the Second Year of B.Sc. (IT). However, the Diploma should be recognized by the Board of Technical Education or any other recognized Government body.

Curriculum: The BSc IT program consists of six semesters. In each semester, students will study five theory and five practical courses. BSc IT program is focused on the following key areas: Programming, Networking, Database Management and Data Analysis, Embedded Systems and IOT,  Internet Security, GIS.

Career Prospects:Successful graduates of B.Sc. IT course interested in further studies in the discipline may go for pursuing M.Sc. IT, MCA, MBA etc. For those interested in pursuing it professionally, Software Development, Software Testing, Networking, Data Analysis, Android Programming are the major areas.

M.Sc I.T

MSc. IT: M.Sc. IT is a 2 years post graduate degree program. During these 2 years, students are given both theoretical as well as practical knowledge in the field of IT (Information Technology).

Curriculum:  The MSc IT program consists of four semesters. In each semester, students will study four subjects.This program is designed to fulfill the latest demand in the field of IT Industry. Major areas of focus would be Cloud Computing, AWS, Digital Image Processing and Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Robotics, Computer Forensics, Ethical Hacking  etc.

Eligibility:  Bachelors’ degree in Information Technology/Computer Science/Physics/Stats/Electronics or equivalent examination from a recognized university.
